Zestimate refers to Zillow's automated valuation model that uses various data sources and algorithms to calculate an approximate value for residential properties in the United States. It takes into account factors such as property details, tax assessments, recent sales data of similar properties in the area, and other relevant information. Neural Zestimate, an upgraded version of Zestimate by training a single national-scale neural network model on many years worth of home details data to make the best estimate possible.
